LG Wonderfall 2010

This project is an interactive water screen I worked on while at Aniframe in 2010. An interactive water screen uses opencv to track subjects in front of the screen and uses computer controlled water nozzles to draw and write images in water. For this project I worked mainly on the technical side. I developed the water screen electronic controller hardware and software, as well as the interactive graphics software and video switching software. Exhibited in Johannesburg, South Africa for LG during the 2010 World Cup
