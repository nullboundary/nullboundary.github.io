Mute 2012

Mute is a club in Itaewon Seoul, Designed by Chiho & Partners. I worked in collaboration with Chiho & Partners and William Craig to create the clubs lighting design, setup and choreography. The club lighting system consists of thousands of RGB leds controlled centrally and dynamically via a DMX network and software systems.
