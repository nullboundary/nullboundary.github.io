Cicada Bluetooth 2014

Cicada BTLE is a development board for prototyping bluetooth devices and connectivity. The platform can be used as wireless control for hardware and a sensor bridge for smart phone apps. The cicada dev board also be easily used for micro locational tracking. Its based on the nordic nRF51822 BTLE Arm SOC. I created the design and pcb layout for this development board for use in my own projects.
