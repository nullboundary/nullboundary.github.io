KT Future Showroom 2010

This project is a pair of multi touch tables I created while working as a creative director at Aniframe Ltd. in 2010. For this project I developed the conceptual idea and basic forms of the tables, how users would interact with them and how they would fit into their environment. I also was part of the technical team assembling and executing the software and hardware setup. These tables were built for use in Korea Telacom's future showroom
