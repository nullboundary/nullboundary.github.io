N0m1 Projects 2011-2013

Nomi Design was a open source hardware electronics company I co-founded in 2011 with Michael Grant. Nomi Design created a large number of prototyping hardware platforms for commercial sale to DIYers and the open source hardware community. Nomi developed platforms such as a multitouch track pad, a 6lowpan wireless module, a servo driver board, and many more. As a founder I worked on all aspects of the projects, from design/conceptualization, circuit design, pcb layout, to embedded software.
