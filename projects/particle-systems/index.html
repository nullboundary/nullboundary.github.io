Particle Systems 2009

This is a series of particle system and flocking system tests I made with Openframeworks in 2009. The tests experiment with a variety of algorithms and techniques to control the particles or boids in the visuals. Some of the tests use live wind data (speed and direction), or user interaction to control the motion of the particles.
