Dotori

Dotori is a wearable prototype device that provides patients with a networked pain- tracking and data aggregation logging service. The device is used to obtain pain feedback so doctors and patients can make informed decisions about treatment. I designed the electronic hardware, restful apis and embedded software. I also participated as a member of the design team.
