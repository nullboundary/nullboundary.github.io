Biography

Noah Shibley is a programmer, designer and hardware developer. He is currently leading the software development of a software context-aware tool at Cicada and has extensive experience developing embedded firmware. He was recently a professor at Tongmyong University teaching digital media and was CTO at a hardware/IoT platform company Nomi Design. With his free time he makes computer graphics visuals for various events around Seoul, contributes to open source software projects on Github and works on hardware experiments involving wireless networks sensor platforms. He received a BFA degree in Art & technology studies at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago and has received a MPS degree from The Interactive Telecommunication Progam (ITP) at New York University.
