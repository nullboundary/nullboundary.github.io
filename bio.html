Biography

Noah Shibley is a designer, programmer and hardware developer currently residing in Seoul. His obsessions include topics such as: Future scenario planning, networked services, wireless networks, electronic hardware platform design, internet connected devices, and sustainable design. With his free time he makes visuals for various events and installations around Seoul using his favorite graphics programming platform Openframeworks. He received a BFA degree in Art & technology studies at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago and has received a MPS degree from The Interactive Telecommunication Progam (ITP) at New York University.
